About Our Company

We are a distinguished merchant exporter with decades of experience, committed to delivering high-quality machinery, raw materials, textiles, food products, spices, pulses, and construction materials to clients across the globe.

Our specialized divisions provide comprehensive turnkey solutions, ensuring seamless project execution and superior operational efficiency:

  • Biscuit Manufacturing Plants - Offering fully and semi-automatic plants, mixers, ovens, packaging lines, automation systems, and complete plant design tailored to client requirements.
  • Plastic & Beverage Plants - Expertise in PET bottle production, juice plants, vacuum and thermoforming machines, and sheet extrusion solutions.
  • Industrial & Miscellaneous Machinery - Supplying corrugated box machinery, welding rods, mosquito repellent coil production lines, exercise book plants, and a wide range of industrial equipment.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, prompt after-sales support, and the capacity to design bespoke solutions, we consistently deliver innovative, reliable, and turnkey industrial solutions to partners across the Middle East and international markets.
