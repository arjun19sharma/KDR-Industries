Engineering Global Industrial Solutions

KDR Industries is a globally focused merchant export and industrial solutions company delivering advanced machinery, turnkey manufacturing plants, industrial materials, and international trade solutions to clients across the world.

Founded in 1992 by Ram Sharma, the company was established with a clear mission that is, to bridge the gap between high-quality manufacturing capabilities and global market demand through dependable exports, engineering expertise, and long-term partnerships.

Over the past three decades, KDR Industries has grown into a trusted name in international trade and industrial solutions, serving clients across diverse industries while maintaining an unwavering commitment to quality, reliability, and innovation.

Today, KDR Industries continues to expand its global reach while remaining grounded in the same values on which it was founded: integrity, excellence, and customer success.

Our Legacy

From its early beginnings as a merchant export enterprise, KDR Industries has steadily evolved into a comprehensive industrial export partner trusted by businesses worldwide.

Over the decades, the company has successfully managed thousands of international shipments and industrial equipment exports, delivering reliable solutions across sectors including manufacturing, food processing, packaging, construction, and engineering.

Our dedicated merchandising and export team ensures efficient sourcing, quality assurance, and seamless logistics, which enables our clients to focus on their core business operations.
